# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers advocate for user experience and usability throughout the project lifecycle. They ensure that solutions are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with customer needs.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and define experience requirements
- Create design mockups, prototypes, and design specifications
- Participate in requirements gathering and feedback loops with Product Managers
- Collaborate with Developers to ensure design feasibility and quality
- Validate user experience through testing and feedback
- Define accessibility and usability standards

### Goals
- Maximize user satisfaction and usability
- Reduce support burden through intuitive design
- Ensure designs are accessible to all users
- Balance user needs with technical and business constraints

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions with the team
- User research findings and usability test results
- Design specifications and component libraries
- Regular collaboration with Product Managers and Developers during planning

### How they interact with existing roles
- Works closely with **Product Managers** to translate customer insights into design requirements
- Collaborates with **Developers** during design and code review to ensure implementation matches design intent
- Partners with **Project Managers** to integrate user testing and design validation into timelines

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers ensure that releases are executed smoothly, safely, and with minimal risk. They coordinate deployment activities, communications, and contingency planning across teams.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and schedule release windows in coordination with stakeholders
- Create and maintain release checklists and deployment runbooks
- Coordinate pre-release testing, validation, and sign-offs
- Manage deployment execution, monitoring, and rollback procedures
- Communicate release status to stakeholders and support teams
- Document lessons learned and improve release processes
- Ensure compliance and audit requirements are met for releases

### Goals
- Execute releases with zero or minimal customer impact
- Reduce release cycle time through process improvements
- Maintain high visibility and communication during critical deployments
- Build confidence in the release process across the organization

### Typical Communication
- Release notes and deployment schedules
- Pre-release coordination meetings
- Real-time deployment status updates
- Post-release retrospectives and metrics

### How they interact with existing roles
- Coordinates with **Project Managers** to integrate release readiness into project timelines
- Works with **Developers** to validate deployment procedures and coordinate hotfixes if needed
- Partners with **Product Managers** to ensure release messaging and rollout strategy align with business goals
- Communicates with support teams and stakeholders on deployment windows and rollback plans

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts provide evidence-based insights to inform decision-making throughout the project lifecycle. They develop metrics, run experiments, and measure outcomes to drive continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track success metrics aligned with project goals
- Develop dashboards and reporting mechanisms for visibility
- Conduct data analysis and experimentation to validate hypotheses
- Identify trends, risks, and opportunities through data exploration
- Support retrospectives with quantitative insights
- Recommend optimizations based on data findings

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision-making across all project phases
- Measure and demonstrate business and customer impact
- Support continuous improvement through metrics and feedback
- Reduce guesswork and gut-based decisions

### Typical Communication
- Metric definitions and success dashboards
- Analysis reports and experiment results
- Retrospective data and improvement recommendations
- Alerts and anomaly notifications for key metrics

### How they interact with existing roles
- Supports **Product Managers** by validating solutions through metrics and user behavior analysis
- Works with **Project Managers** to track progress against success criteria and identify performance trends
- Partners with **Developers** to instrument code for observability and performance measurement
- Participates in retrospectives to provide quantitative data on process improvements

---

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
Support Leads represent the voice of the customer and support teams in project planning and execution. They identify customer pain points, communicate known issues, and escalate blockers that impact customers.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and prioritize customer feedback and support issues
- Communicate known issues and workarounds to stakeholders
- Participate in planning to identify support implications early
- Escalate critical customer-impacting blockers
- Work with Developers on issue triage and hotfixes
- Provide post-release support coordination and customer communication
- Contribute insights to retrospectives on customer impact and satisfaction

### Goals
- Minimize customer support burden and improve satisfaction
- Identify product gaps and improvement opportunities from customer perspective
- Ensure customer voice is heard in planning and execution
- Reduce time-to-resolution for critical customer issues

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries and support volume reports
- Known issues and escalation alerts
- Post-release support readiness and customer communication plans
- Retrospective insights on customer impact

### How they interact with existing roles
- Partners with **Product Managers** to surface customer feedback and identify high-impact improvements
- Collaborates with **Developers** on issue triage, root cause analysis, and hotfix coordination
- Works with **Project Managers** to ensure customer support considerations are integrated into timelines and rollout plans
- Advises **Release Managers** on support readiness and known issues before deployment

- When planning projects, consider which personas are needed based on project scope and complexity.
- Use the interaction descriptions to inform cross-functional communication and collaboration patterns.
